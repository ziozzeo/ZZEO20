IMPERIAL
FASHION
Imperial is an italian fashion brand born in 1978 from entrepreneurs Adriano Aere and Emilia Giberti.

Nowadays the company employs a staff of over 300 collaborators and distributes in 1,500 monobrand and multi-brand shops all over the world. Imperial Fashion combines an international vision with a forward-thinking approach to new trends.

Its main strength is the ability to ride the latest trends, being able to rapidly create impressive models and collections and their constant research enables them to meet the taste of the most demanding fashionists, without losing sight of style and attention to details.
