SEPTEMBER 2019 — PERSONAL WEBSITE
ZZ-EO.COM
2019 EDITION
My first ever digital portfolio was an hard challenge for me.
I tried not to follow the usual trends and make a totally experimental one by myself, picturing a brand new personal identity (mainly visible from the predominant violet color and a new logo, the rotating face) and building a sort of visible grid that lays all the website content.

The font choice fell on Termina by Fort Foundry for the main titles and Neue Haas Unica by Monotype for the normal text.
